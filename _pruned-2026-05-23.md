## CUT 17 — Payments routing: decision tree, not Square-absolutism

- **Why:** Rule painted Stripe-for-accept-money as a sin. For genuine recurring SaaS billing with seat-based + Entitlements + Tax, Stripe Billing IS the right rail.
- **Where:** `~/.agentskills/rules/payments-routing.md`, `~/.agentskills/CLAUDE.md` Stack, `~/.claude/CLAUDE.md` Site Mode rule 6
- **Square (default for):** donations, POS, e-commerce, sub-$100 tickets, nonprofit recurring, hybrid in-person+online
- **Stripe Billing (right rail when):** ≥2 of [seat-based / usage-metered / Entitlements / net-30 invoicing / multi-currency / tax-rate complexity]
- **Stripe Connect Express:** payouts to contractors/vendors/volunteers (unchanged)
- **Revert:** restore "Square = accepting money. Stripe = sending money. Never reverse this." absolutism.

## Bonus — CUT 5 (i18n auto-fire narrowed)

- **Why:** Auto-fired on any "physical service area" — fired on national SaaS, generated `/es/*` mirrors nobody asked for, broke hreflang.
- **Where:** `~/.agentskills/rules/i18n-by-demographics.md` § Rule (trigger conditions)
- **New trigger (ALL FOUR required):**
  1. Org has a defined **local physical service area** (not national, not online-only)

- **Manual opt-in:** orgs that want mirrors despite not meeting all 4 → `_research.json.i18n_opt_in = ['es','pt']`
- **Preserved:** route shape, hreflang, translation pipeline, locale-mapping table, reference incident.
- **Revert:** restore broad "any entity with a defined geography" trigger.
